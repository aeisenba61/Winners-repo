Where's Ronald and What's He Up To

Visualizing links between McDonalds locations, health, and poverty

McDonalds has become a ubiquitous part of the American landscape, with almost 106 million people eating McDonalds within the past month. However, research shows that greater fast food consuption is linked with negative health outcomes such as obesity and diabetes. Low income individuals are also at heightened risk for obesity, in part because of greater availability of fast food and lower availability of healthy food.
